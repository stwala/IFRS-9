Factor Models Component

Our software supports dynamic Factor Models tailored to the three key stages of credit risk classification under IFRS 9. These models use macroeconomic variables, borrower characteristics, and exposure-level data to estimate Expected Credit Losses (ECL) at different stages of deterioration.

How Factor Models Work

  • Integrate macroeconomic variables for scenario-based ECL estimation.
  • Adapt to borrower-specific characteristics and exposure-level data.
  • Support all three IFRS 9 stages: performing, underperforming, and non-performing.

Key Benefits

  • Flexible and modular model design.
  • Transparent methodology for regulatory compliance.
  • Scalable for portfolios of any size.
